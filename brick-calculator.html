Bricklaying Tips

📏 Accurate Measurements

Measure wall dimensions carefully. Account for door and window openings. Use a story pole for consistent courses.

🧱 Brick Selection

Buy all bricks from the same lot for color consistency. Inspect for cracks, chips, and uniform size.

💧 Mortar Preparation

Mix mortar in small batches. Use within 2 hours. Keep mortar damp and workable throughout the day.

📐 Level and Plumb

Check level frequently with a spirit level. Use string lines for straight courses. Maintain consistent joint thickness.

🌡️ Weather Conditions

Don't lay bricks in freezing weather. Protect fresh work from rain. Allow adequate curing time.

🛠️ Tools Needed

Essential tools: trowel, level, string line, jointer, brick hammer, and measuring tape. Invest in quality tools.
