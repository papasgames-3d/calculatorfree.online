Area Calculation Tips

📏 Consistent Units

Always use the same units for all measurements. The result will be in square units (e.g., m² for meters).

📐 Measuring Techniques

For irregular shapes, break them down into simpler shapes and add their areas together.

🔢 Precision

Use more decimal places for precise calculations, especially for circles where π is involved.

📊 Real Applications

Area calculations are useful for flooring, painting, gardening, construction, and material estimation.
