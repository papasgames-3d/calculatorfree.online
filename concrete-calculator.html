Concrete Project Tips

📏 Accurate Measurements

Always measure twice and order 5-10% extra concrete to account for waste and slight variations in depth.

🌡️ Weather Considerations

Don't pour concrete in freezing temperatures or during rain. Ideal temperature is 50-70°F.

💧 Proper Curing

Keep concrete moist for at least 7 days after pouring for maximum strength development.

🏗️ Base Preparation

Proper base preparation with gravel and compaction is crucial for long-lasting concrete.

⚡ Quick Setting

Concrete starts setting in 30-60 minutes, so have your tools ready and work efficiently.

🔨 Reinforcement

Consider rebar or wire mesh for slabs over 4 inches thick or in areas with heavy loads.
