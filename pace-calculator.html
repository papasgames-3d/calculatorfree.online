Training Tips

🏃‍♂️ Easy Runs (Zone 1)

70-80% of training should be easy pace. You should be able to hold a conversation while running.

⚡ Tempo Runs (Zone 3)

Comfortably hard pace for 20-40 minutes. Improves lactate threshold and race fitness.

🔥 Interval Training (Zone 4-5)

Short, intense efforts with recovery. Improves VO2 max and running economy.

📊 Progressive Runs

Start easy and gradually increase pace. Great for building fitness and race simulation.
