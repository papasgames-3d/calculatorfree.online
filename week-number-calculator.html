About ISO 8601 Week Numbers

ISO 8601 Standard
Week numbers follow the ISO 8601 international standard where weeks start on Monday and the first week contains January 4th.
Week Numbering
Most years have 52 weeks, but some years have 53 weeks. Week 1 is the first week with at least 4 days in the new year.
Business Use
Week numbers are commonly used in business for planning, scheduling, and reporting across different countries and industries.
International Standard
ISO 8601 is recognized worldwide and ensures consistent week numbering across different systems and countries.
