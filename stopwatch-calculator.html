How to Use the Stopwatch Calculator

Basic Timing

Click Start to begin timing, Pause to stop temporarily, and Reset to clear the timer. The display shows hours, minutes, seconds, and milliseconds.

Lap Timing

Use the Lap button while the timer is running to record split times. Perfect for tracking multiple rounds, laps, or intervals.

Statistics

View detailed statistics including fastest lap, slowest lap, average lap time, and total number of laps recorded.

Export Data

Export your lap times to CSV, JSON, copy to clipboard, or print results for record keeping and analysis.
