Tile Installation Tips

📏 Plan Your Layout

Start from the center and work outward. Use chalk lines to mark your starting point and maintain straight rows.

🔧 Prepare the Surface

Ensure surface is clean, level, and dry. Use primer if needed. Fix any cracks or uneven areas before tiling.

💧 Waterproofing

Use waterproof membrane in wet areas like showers. Proper waterproofing prevents costly damage later.

⏰ Work in Sections

Work in small sections to prevent adhesive from drying out. Have all tools ready before starting each section.

🎯 Use Tile Spacers

Consistent spacing creates professional results. Remove spacers before grout sets completely.

🧽 Clean as You Go

Clean excess adhesive and grout immediately. Use damp sponge and change water frequently during grouting.
