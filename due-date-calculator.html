Pregnancy Tips & Care

Prenatal Vitamins

Take prenatal vitamins with folic acid daily. Start before conception if possible to prevent neural tube defects.

Healthy Nutrition

Eat a balanced diet rich in fruits, vegetables, whole grains, and lean proteins. Avoid raw fish, deli meats, and unpasteurized foods.

Safe Exercise

Stay active with pregnancy-safe exercises like walking, swimming, and prenatal yoga. Consult your doctor before starting new routines.

Adequate Rest

Get 7-9 hours of sleep nightly. Use pregnancy pillows for comfort and sleep on your side, preferably left side.

Regular Checkups

Attend all prenatal appointments. Regular monitoring ensures both mother and baby's health throughout pregnancy.

Avoid Harmful Substances

No smoking, alcohol, or drugs. Limit caffeine to 200mg daily. Check with doctor before taking any medications.
