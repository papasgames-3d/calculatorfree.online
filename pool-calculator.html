Pool Maintenance Tips

🧪 Chemical Balance

Maintain pH 7.2-7.6, total alkalinity 80-120 ppm, and chlorine 1-3 ppm for optimal water quality.

🌊 Water Testing

Test water 2-3 times per week during swimming season. Test more frequently in hot weather.

🧽 Regular Cleaning

Skim debris daily, vacuum weekly, and brush walls to prevent algae buildup.

🔄 Circulation

Run pool pump 8-12 hours daily to ensure proper water circulation and filtration.

🍃 Seasonal Care

Shock pool weekly during peak season. Reduce chemical usage and runtime in cooler months.

⚗️ Chemical Storage

Store chemicals in cool, dry place away from direct sunlight. Never mix different chemicals.
