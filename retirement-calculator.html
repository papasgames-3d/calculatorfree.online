Retirement Planning Tips

  • Start saving early - compound interest is your best friend
  • Aim to save at least 10-15% of your income for retirement
  • Take advantage of employer 401(k) matching programs
  • Consider increasing contributions when you get a raise
  • Diversify your retirement portfolio across different asset classes
  • Review and adjust your retirement plan annually
  • Consider the impact of inflation on your purchasing power
  • Plan for healthcare costs in retirement
