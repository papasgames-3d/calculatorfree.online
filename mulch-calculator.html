Mulching Tips & Guidelines

📏 Proper Depth

Apply 2-4 inches for most plants. Too thick can suffocate roots, too thin won't control weeds effectively.

🌳 Tree Mulching

Keep mulch 6 inches away from tree trunks to prevent rot and pest problems. Create a donut shape, not a volcano.

⏰ Best Timing

Apply mulch in late spring after soil warms up, or in fall before winter. Refresh annually or as needed.

🌿 Plant Spacing

Pull mulch back from plant stems and crowns to prevent moisture-related diseases and pest issues.

💧 Watering Considerations

Water before applying mulch. Dry mulch can repel water, so ensure soil is moist underneath.

🔄 Maintenance

Rake and fluff mulch periodically to prevent matting. Add fresh layer annually to maintain depth.
