Fuel Efficiency Tips

🚗 Driving Habits

Maintain steady speeds, avoid rapid acceleration and hard braking. Use cruise control on highways.

🔧 Vehicle Maintenance

Keep tires properly inflated, change air filters regularly, and get tune-ups as scheduled.

📦 Reduce Weight

Remove unnecessary items from your car. Every 100 pounds can reduce efficiency by 1-2%.

🌡️ Temperature Control

Use A/C efficiently. At low speeds, windows down is better. At highway speeds, use A/C.

🛣️ Route Planning

Combine errands into one trip. Plan routes to avoid heavy traffic and construction zones.

⛽ Fuel Quality

Use the recommended octane level. Higher octane doesn't improve efficiency unless required.
