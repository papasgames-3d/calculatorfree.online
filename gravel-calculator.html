Gravel Installation Tips

📏 Accurate Measurements

Measure carefully and account for irregular shapes. Use string to outline the area for complex layouts.

🏗️ Site Preparation

Clear vegetation and level the area. Install landscape fabric to prevent weeds and mixing with soil.

📐 Proper Depth

Use 2" for light foot traffic, 3-4" for vehicle traffic. Deeper is better for high-use areas.

🛤️ Edging Installation

Install edging material to contain gravel and maintain clean borders. Metal or plastic edging works well.

💧 Drainage Considerations

Ensure proper slope for drainage (2% grade). Consider drainage needs for your climate and soil type.

🚛 Delivery Planning

Ensure delivery truck access. Order 10% extra for settling and future maintenance needs.
