Frequently Asked Questions

How do I calculate my exact age? +
Simply enter your complete birth date (day, month, year) in the first calculator. The tool will automatically compute and display your current age in years, months, and days, along with a detailed breakdown.
Can I calculate my age at a specific date? +
Yes! Use the second calculator to enter your birth date and select any target date. This will show you exactly how old you were or will be on that specific date.
Is my personal information stored or shared? +
No, absolutely not. All calculations are performed locally in your browser. We don't store, collect, or share any personal information including your birth date. Your privacy is completely protected.
What zodiac sign information is provided? +
The calculator automatically determines your zodiac sign based on your birth date and displays it along with your age results. This includes both the symbol and name of your astrological sign.
