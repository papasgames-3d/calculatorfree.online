❓ Frequently Asked Questions

How do I calculate the difference between two times? +
Enter your start time and end time in the "Calculate Time Difference" section using hours, minutes, and seconds fields, then click "Calculate Difference". The tool will show you the exact difference with a detailed breakdown.
Can I add or subtract time from a specific time? +
Yes! Use the "Add/Subtract Time" calculator. Enter your base time and the time you want to add or subtract, then select the operation (Add or Subtract). The result will show the new time, handling overflow automatically.
What time units can I convert between? +
Our time converter supports 7 different units: seconds, minutes, hours, days, weeks, months, and years. You can convert from any unit to any other unit with high precision.
How does the working hours calculator work? +
Enter your work start time, end time, break duration, and hourly rate. The calculator will compute your total working hours, regular hours, overtime hours, and estimated earnings. It also handles overnight shifts automatically.
Is my data stored when I use this calculator? +
No, absolutely not. All calculations are performed locally in your browser. We don't store, collect, or share any of your time or salary information. Your privacy is completely protected.
Can I use this calculator on my mobile device? +
Yes! Our hours calculator is fully responsive and works perfectly on smartphones, tablets, and desktop computers. The interface automatically adjusts to provide the best experience on your device.
How accurate are the time calculations? +
Our calculator uses precise JavaScript algorithms that handle all time complexities including overflow, underflow, and format conversions. The results are mathematically accurate and reliable for all your time calculation needs.
