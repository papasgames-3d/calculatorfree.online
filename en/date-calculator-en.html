❓ Frequently Asked Questions

How do I calculate the difference between two dates? +
Simply select your start date and end date using the date pickers in the "Calculate Date Difference" section, then click "Calculate Difference". The tool will show you the exact difference in years, months, weeks, days, hours, and minutes.
Can I add or subtract days from a specific date? +
Yes! Use the "Add/Subtract Days" calculator. Enter your base date and the number of days you want to add (positive number) or subtract (negative number). The result will show the new date along with the day of the week.
What are working days and how are they calculated? +
Working days are business days that exclude weekends (Saturday and Sunday). Our calculator counts only Monday through Friday within your selected date range, making it perfect for project planning and business calculations.
Is my data stored or shared when I use this calculator? +
No, absolutely not. All calculations are performed locally in your browser. We don't store, collect, or share any of your date information. Your privacy is completely protected.
Can I use this calculator on my mobile device? +
Yes! Our date calculator is fully responsive and works perfectly on smartphones, tablets, and desktop computers. The interface automatically adjusts to provide the best experience on your device.
How accurate are the date calculations? +
Our calculator uses precise JavaScript Date algorithms that account for leap years, varying month lengths, and all calendar complexities. The results are mathematically accurate and reliable for all your date calculation needs.
What date range does the calculator support? +
The calculator supports a wide range of dates from the early 1900s to well into the future (2100+). This covers virtually all practical date calculation needs for personal, business, and historical purposes.
