Frequently Asked Questions

How do I add two time periods? +
Enter the first and second time values in their respective fields (hours, minutes, seconds), then click the "Add" button. The result will be displayed immediately.
Does the tool support negative time calculations? +
Yes, when subtracting time and the result is negative, the tool will display the result with a negative sign to indicate that the second time is greater than the first time.
Can I use this tool on my mobile phone? +
Yes, the tool is designed to be responsive and works well on all devices: computers, tablets, and mobile phones.
What is the maximum time value I can enter? +
There's no strict limit for hours in the converter, but for the add/subtract calculator, hours are limited to 0-23, minutes and seconds to 0-59 for standard time format.
Is my data saved or stored anywhere? +
No, all calculations are performed locally in your browser. No data is sent to any server or stored anywhere. Your privacy is completely protected.
