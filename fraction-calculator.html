Fraction Tips

  • Always simplify fractions to their lowest terms
  • To add/subtract fractions, find a common denominator
  • To multiply fractions, multiply numerators and denominators
  • To divide fractions, multiply by the reciprocal
  • Use the GCD (Greatest Common Divisor) to simplify fractions
  • Mixed numbers should be converted to improper fractions first
