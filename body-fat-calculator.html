Body Fat Health Tips

  • Body fat percentage is more accurate than BMI for assessing health
  • Essential fat is necessary for basic physical and physiological health
  • Athletes typically have lower body fat percentages
  • Combine cardio and strength training to reduce body fat
  • Focus on a balanced diet with adequate protein
  • Stay hydrated and get adequate sleep for optimal metabolism
  • Consult a healthcare professional for personalized advice
  • Track progress over time rather than focusing on single measurements
