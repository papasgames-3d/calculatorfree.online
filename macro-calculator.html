Macro Tracking Tips

📱 Use Tracking Apps

MyFitnessPal, Cronometer, or Lose It help track macros accurately. Weigh food for precision.

🍽️ Meal Prep

Prepare meals in advance to ensure you hit your macro targets consistently throughout the week.

⚡ Timing Matters

Eat protein throughout the day, carbs around workouts, and don't fear healthy fats.

💧 Stay Hydrated

Drink plenty of water, especially when increasing protein intake. Aim for 0.5-1 oz per pound of body weight.

📊 Track Progress

Monitor body composition changes, not just weight. Take measurements and progress photos.

🔄 Adjust as Needed

Macros aren't set in stone. Adjust based on results, energy levels, and how you feel.
