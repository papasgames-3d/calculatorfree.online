About Unit Conversion

Unit conversion is the process of converting the value of a physical quantity from one unit to another. This tool supports conversion between various measurement systems including:

  • Metric System: Based on powers of 10 (meters, grams, liters, etc.)
  • Imperial System: Traditional British units (feet, pounds, gallons, etc.)
  • US Customary: American variations of imperial units
  • Scientific Units: SI base units and derived units

Common Conversion Factors:

  • 1 meter = 3.28084 feet
  • 1 kilogram = 2.20462 pounds
  • 1 liter = 0.264172 gallons (US)
  • °F = (°C × 9/5) + 32
  • 1 square meter = 10.7639 square feet
