Debt Management Tips

🎯 Choose Your Strategy

Debt Avalanche saves more money, but Debt Snowball provides psychological wins. Pick what motivates you.

💰 Find Extra Money

Cut expenses, sell items, or take on side work to increase your debt payments. Every extra dollar helps.

🚫 Stop Creating New Debt

Put credit cards away and use cash or debit cards only while paying off existing debt.

📞 Negotiate with Creditors

Call your creditors to request lower interest rates or payment plans. Many are willing to work with you.

📊 Track Your Progress

Monitor your debt balances monthly and celebrate milestones to stay motivated on your journey.

🏦 Consider Consolidation

A consolidation loan might lower your interest rate and simplify payments, but compare carefully.
