Drywall Installation Tips

📏 Measuring Accurately

Measure twice, cut once. Account for electrical outlets, switches, and fixtures when planning layout.

🔧 Proper Fastening

Use 1¼" screws for ½" drywall on wood studs. Space screws 12" on center for walls, 8" for ceilings.

🎯 Joint Finishing

Apply 3 coats of compound: embedding, filling, and finishing. Sand lightly between coats.

📐 Sheet Orientation

Install sheets horizontally when possible to minimize joints and improve strength.

💡 Tool Requirements

Essential tools: utility knife, T-square, screw gun, taping knives, and sanding blocks.

🌡️ Environmental Conditions

Maintain temperature 55-70°F and humidity below 70% during installation and drying.
