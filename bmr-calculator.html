Metabolism Boosting Tips

  • Build muscle mass through strength training - muscle burns more calories at rest
  • Stay hydrated - dehydration can slow your metabolism
  • Eat protein with every meal - it has a higher thermic effect
  • Get adequate sleep - poor sleep can disrupt metabolic hormones
  • Don't skip meals - this can slow your metabolism
  • Include spicy foods - they can temporarily boost metabolism
  • Drink green tea - it contains metabolism-boosting compounds
  • Stay active throughout the day - even small movements help
