Date Calculation Tips

  • Business days typically exclude weekends (Saturday and Sunday)
  • Leap years occur every 4 years, except for years divisible by 100 but not 400
  • February has 28 days in regular years and 29 days in leap years
  • Use ISO format (YYYY-MM-DD) for international date standards
  • Time zones can affect date calculations for global applications
  • Consider holidays when calculating business days for specific regions
