Auto Loan Tips

  • Shop around for the best interest rates from banks, credit unions, and dealers
  • Consider a larger down payment to reduce monthly payments and total interest
  • Check your credit score before applying - higher scores get better rates
  • Don't forget to factor in insurance, taxes, and registration fees
  • Consider certified pre-owned vehicles for better value
  • Shorter loan terms mean higher monthly payments but less total interest
  • Get pre-approved for financing before shopping to know your budget
