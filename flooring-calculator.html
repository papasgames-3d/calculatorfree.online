Flooring Installation Tips

📏 Accurate Measurements

Measure each room carefully. For irregular shapes, break into rectangles and add together. Always add 5-20% waste factor.

🏠 Consider Your Lifestyle

High-traffic areas need durable flooring. Pets and children may require waterproof or scratch-resistant options.

💧 Moisture Considerations

Avoid hardwood in bathrooms and basements. Use tile, luxury vinyl, or engineered wood in moisture-prone areas.

📦 Order Extra Material

Keep 5-10% extra flooring for future repairs. Dye lots can vary, so having matching material is important.

🔧 Professional vs DIY

Laminate and vinyl are DIY-friendly. Hardwood and tile often require professional installation for best results.

💰 Budget Planning

Remember to include underlayment, trim, transitions, and removal of old flooring in your budget calculations.
